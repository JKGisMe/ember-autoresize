Ember.AutoResize

A mixin for Ember Views designed to be a drop-in

Dependecies

You will need to include jquery, handlebars, ember.js and the autoresize mixin.

Usage

Ember.TextField and Ember.TextArea work out of the box with Ember.AutoResize. Simply add the attribute autoresize to your view to add the magic:

<script type="text/x-handlebars" data-template-name="index">
            {{view Ember.TextArea autoresize=true value="I resize vertically!" maxHeight=500}}
            {{view Ember.TextField autoresize=true value="I resize horizontally!" maxWidth=500}}
            </script>
